Hello there! I'm Hugh.

I'm a recent computer science graduate with a keen interest in technology, productivity, and problem-solving. I have spent a lifetime learning and cultivating a broad range of interests. This personal website will be my chance to share everything I know and love.

Who am I?

As a recent graduate, a big part of my identity is that of a student. And like most students, I am sometimes worried about being too inexperienced to call myself a "real" software developer. Fortunately, I'm steadfast and ambitious. As such, as I steadily transition into a professional role, I aim to grow out of these insecurities and sculpt myself into a competent programmer.

My dream has always been to work at a large company where I have the chance to learn from great industry professionals. A big goal for me is to prove myself and stand proudly amongst my peers; I want to be recognized for my efforts and admired for my ability within the craft. A lofty goal, I know, but it's an honest one.

So while l know that I'm just a growing bud in this industry, it won't stop me. It only makes me more excited to see what my future has in store for me.

Philosophy

First and foremost, I am a problem solver. I develop solutions and bring value to my clients. To do that, I always have an array of tools that are ready to use. My favourite? Web Technologies. At the moment, I am in love with front-end development; I find it to be the perfect blend of design and logic.

Secondly, I fix things. The unfortunate reality is that things break, like, all the time. Our focus as developers isn't to just create new things but also maintain them. It's one of the skills I strongly recommend every programmer practices - it is our job to fix things when they break.
